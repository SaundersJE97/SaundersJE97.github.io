About me

I am currently pursuing a PhD in computer science at the University of Bath. My research investigates collision avoidance systems for delivery drones. I hope to utilise camera based sensors to detect obstacles and then, using deep reinforcement learning, train a neural network for collision avoidance.

My research interests include:
Drone hardware, Autonomous Vehicle Systems, Reinforcement Learning, Explainable Reinforcement Learning and Computer Vision

My research is sponsored by the UKRI Center of Doctoral College in Accountable, Responsible and Transparent AI.
